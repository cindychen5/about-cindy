About Cindy

This is about Cindy.

I was born and raised in NYC. I attended Northeastern University in Boston, MA and graduated with a doctorate in pharmacy. After 10 years in Boston, I recently moved to Albuquerque in September to be closer to family -- my fiance is originally from Albuquerque. I enjoy biking, hiking, running and trying new food. It's been a little hard to feel out the food scene here due to the pandemic but I have been able to explore the beer scene here. Some breweries I have been to include Bow and Arrow, Bosque, Steelbender, Kactus and Marble.
