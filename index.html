About Cindy

This is about Cindy, the most tech-savvy non-drug beer-lovin' gal west of the Mississippi.

I'm a city gal, born and raised in the Big Apple. I like drugs, a lot. That's why I moved to Albuquerque. Because I wanted to be nearer to Walter White. But then, after moving here in September, I realized that you can't get close to Walter, mostly because of Covid, but also because he's an elusive mo'fo. (I'm from NYC, we talk like that.)

So, I decided to shack up with this dude that I've actually known for a long time, and now I've got a rock on my finger (not the Walter White kind of rock). He's from the 'Turkey (I can talk like that because I'm a local now), so his whole family is here and I thought his family might not like my previous profession, so, here I am! Getting out of drugs and into ...uhhh....this. I might need drugs to get through it.

Also, I like beer (which is sort of a drug) and La Cumbre is my favorite beer. Hands down. Their Hefeweizen is nice for the morning, fruity, you know? And I prefer their Elevated IPA to get me through the afternoon, the stiff stuff. My daily stash is pictured above. I really like New Mexico now. (I also like the Oxford comma.)
